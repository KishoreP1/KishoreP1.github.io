Our Product

The design team has carefully researched and engineered an efficient and safe shower

Perfectly Design

The choice of materials that include ABS injection molded base and seat, a aluminum gas lift and waterproof hinges all aid in both the functionality of the product, as well as the durability and comfortability.

Carefully Planned

The product consists of a 15 inch height, diameter of 10 inches and a width of 7 inches, specifically designed to be compact so that users can easily bring it wherever they need.

Well Built

The product is designed to withstand a drop test of ~4 feet, so that if it were to slip out of ones hand while carrying it, there will be no damages that affect the functionality of the device.

PortaStable

Providing a safe, stable, portable, and easy to use shower chair.
