Our Mission

Provide a more effective and portable means of stabilization while showering to achieve greater time efficiency, improved ease of daily life and an overall safer experience.

Safety

The team strives to provide a product that improves safety for users in the shower, as the stability improvement decreases the risk of injury from a fall.

Time Efficiency

Time efficiency is pivotal because we strive to ensure users are spending the time with people they love, rather than on tedious tasks in their daily lives.

Portability

While many might have solutions in place in their home, the team has an emphasis on portability so the users will have similar, safe and effective experiences in the shower no matter their location.

Ease Of Use

Ease of use is a major focal point for the team to optimize user experience, as we strive to make a product that is super simple to use so that the user can enjoy it in a quick and easy fashion.
